# Presentation Writer Skill

Use this skill when creating a PowerPoint, slide deck, executive presentation, technical presentation, roadmap deck, investor deck, or product presentation.

Output format:
## Slide 1: Title
- Bullet
- Bullet
- Bullet

## Slide 2: Title
- Bullet
- Bullet
- Bullet

Rules:
- Create 8 to 12 slides unless specified otherwise.
- Each slide must have a clear executive title.
- Use 3 to 5 concise bullets per slide.
- Avoid dense paragraphs.
- Tell a logical story.
- Do not invent facts.
